## Our Mission

We make cryptoasset investigations faster, deeper, and broader — producing evidence that holds up in court. Our platform is built on open-source foundations and grounded in a decade of peer-reviewed research.

## Our Story

Iknaio was founded in 2021 as a spin-off of the <a href="https://csh.ac.at" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Complexity Science Hub Vienna</a>. Our roots go back to 2015, when <a href="https://bernhardhaslhofer.info" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Bernhard Haslhofer</a> and <a href="https://www.ait.ac.at/en/about-the-ait/researcher-profiles/detail/king-ross" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Ross King</a> initiated the <a href="https://graphsense.org" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GraphSense</a> research project at <a href="https://www.ait.ac.at" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">AIT Austrian Institute of Technology</a>.

Over the following decade, publicly funded research turned GraphSense into one of the best-established open-source cryptoasset analytics platforms available today. We founded Iknaio to bring that research to operational customers who require tools built on transparent, auditable, scientific foundations.

## Security and Compliance

All production systems are hosted on EU-based infrastructure in Austria and Germany. No data leaves the European Union. We process only publicly available blockchain data, customer-contributed data under controlled engagement, or trusted third-party sources under explicit agreement.

We are working toward ISO 27001 certification. Our platform processes blockchain data only; personal data handling is limited to customer account management and is governed by our <a href="../privacy/" rel="noopener">Privacy Policy</a>.

For questions about our security practices or to request our Data Processing Agreement, contact us at <a href="mailto:contact@iknaio.com">contact@iknaio.com</a>.
