Iknaio is actively participating in the Tokyo kickoff event for Project ViCTOR, joining "over 60 delegates hailing from over 10 prominent Asian countries." The company expresses appreciation to Interpol for organizing the event and to Japan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs for hosting. This participation reflects the organization's "commitment to innovation and collaboration in the Asian market."
