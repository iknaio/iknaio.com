Our co-founder Bernhard Haslhofer serves on the Organizing Committee for the [4th International Cryptoasset Analytics Workshop (CAAW)](https://caaw.io/2025/), scheduled for April 18, 2025 in Miyakojima, Japan.

CAAW 2025 welcomes researchers across academic disciplines to share current research on cryptoassets and their ecosystems. The workshop will facilitate discussions on analytics methods spanning multiple layers—P2P networks, consensus models, and both on-chain and off-chain data—while addressing legal, ethical, privacy, and security considerations.

The event is co-located with Financial Cryptography and Data Security 2025, featuring both invited presentations and peer-reviewed research talks. Presentation topics may encompass empirical studies, analytical tools, case studies, and datasets offering comprehensive perspectives on cryptoasset analytics.

Through his role on the Organizing Committee, Bernhard helps shape the agenda and foster collaboration among academics in this field, providing an opportunity to advance the discipline and examine current challenges and opportunities in cryptoasset evolution and analytics.
