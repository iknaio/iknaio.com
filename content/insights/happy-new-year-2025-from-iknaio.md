As we step into 2025, we take a moment to reflect on the progress made and the challenges ahead. At Iknaio, we are dedicated to providing robust solutions that address the complexities of blockchain analysis across both public and private sectors.

In 2024, we expanded our capabilities to better support **public authorities**, including law enforcement and investigative bodies. With tools like **Pathfinder 2.0** and **CaseConnect**, we empower agencies to analyze blockchain transactions with precision, trace illicit activities, and build actionable insights for their cases. Our **automated workflows** help streamline processes, enabling faster and more reliable outcomes.

For **private institutions**, our solutions are designed to enhance transparency, improve efficiency, and ensure compliance in an increasingly digital financial landscape.

Looking to 2025, our **mission** remains focused on creating secure and transparent blockchain ecosystems while equipping investigators, regulators and the financial institutions with the tools they need to combat financial crime effectively.

We extend our gratitude to our clients and partners for their trust and collaboration in 2024. Here's to another year of advancing innovation and building safer digital infrastructures together.

Wishing you a healthy, successful, and impactful 2025!
