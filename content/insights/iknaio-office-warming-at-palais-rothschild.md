On May 8th, Iknaio officially opened its new office in the **Palais Rothschild** in Vienna. The company hosted an evening celebration with friends, partners, customers, and colleagues to mark this significant milestone.

The event featured networking opportunities and remarks toasting Iknaio's future, set within the historic elegance of the venue. The company expressed gratitude to attendees and acknowledged its team's dedication and hard work in making the move possible.

According to the announcement, "This office move represents much more than just a change of location, it's a symbol of Iknaio's continued growth, ambition, and commitment to delivering cutting-edge solutions in cryptoasset forensics, risk monitoring and compliance."

The company concluded by inviting the community to continue the journey together into what it describes as the next chapter of growth and innovation.
