## Uncovering Cryptoasset Abuse in the Dark Web

The next session of the Innovation Seminar Series arrives on **January 14th, 2025**, focusing on a critical issue in cryptoasset investigations: uncovering abuse within the dark web.

In collaboration with [**CFLW**](https://cflw.com), this session will showcase advanced methodologies to assist investigators in closing data gaps, validating questionable activity, and improving investigative outcomes. The session targets professionals seeking to sharpen their investigative expertise and surmount typical obstacles in dark web research.

### What You'll Learn

- **Advanced Techniques:** Learn innovative methods for spotting and evaluating cryptoasset misuse on the dark web.
- **Practical Solutions:** Obtain actionable guidance for resolving cases with greater assurance and stronger evidentiary backing.
- **Collaborative Knowledge:** Build connections with worldwide specialists addressing comparable investigative obstacles.
