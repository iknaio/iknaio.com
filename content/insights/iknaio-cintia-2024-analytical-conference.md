Our CEO, Karl Zettl, had the honor of speaking at the CINTIA 2024 Analytical Conference in Warsaw, hosted by the [Polish Platform for Homeland Security](https://ppbw.pl/en/bringing-together-security-stakeholders-cintia-2024-analytical-conference/).

This event brought together stakeholders from across the security landscape to exchange ideas and discuss the latest advancements in analytical methodologies and tools.

Karl shared insights on the critical role of Blockchain analysis and Iknaio's approach in combating financial crime in the digital age. As the crypto ecosystem continues to evolve, collaboration and innovation remain key to addressing emerging threats effectively.

We're proud to have been part of this vital conversation and to contribute to the dialogue shaping the future of security and risk management.

A big thank you to the organizers and everyone who engaged in the discussions—together, we're paving the way for safer digital ecosystems.
